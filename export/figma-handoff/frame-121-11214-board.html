Behavioural modes

Care

Reduce cognitive load — softer contrast, more open layout, low-intensity motion and waveform.

Regulation

Maintain balance — neutral tone, steady rhythm, default-centred sliders.

Performance

Enable clarity — sharper hierarchy, slightly tighter density, restrained snappy motion.

Principles

  • Sound leads · Motion supports
  • Restraint over expression
  • Clarity over personality
  • One palette, adaptive behaviour
  • Brand and product connected
