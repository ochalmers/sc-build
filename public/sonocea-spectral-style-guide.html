Spectral palette

Dark: near-black through deep slate for layered depth; mist for tertiary chrome.

Spectral accents

Controlled pastel spectrum in light mode; luminous accents with subtle glow in dark mode.

Support tones

Earthy anchors for labels, charts, and secondary affordances.

Atmospheric gradients

Use as hero washes, card footers, or modal scrims—keep blur soft and transitions gradual.
